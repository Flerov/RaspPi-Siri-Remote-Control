|> In order for everything to work properly, you need a Google Email (Gmail) account, in addition you need to tick under
   'Settings' > 'Notes' > 'Standard account', 'Gmail' on your iPhone.
   For that you have to go under 'Settings' > 'Accounts & Passwords' > 'Accounts'.
   Here you have to add your Gmail-Account
   Activate 'Mail' and 'Notes' in the same tab.
  > Now you are ready to go.


Manual.
>Start
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
-                                                                                                            -
- 1.Open a shell                                                                                             -
- 2.Navigate to your Siri-Folder.                                                                            -
- 3.Start 'Main.py' from your shell.                                                                         -
- 4.Follow the instructions                                                                                  -
- 5.After everything was configured correctly start 'Main.py' again from your shell.                         -
-   (only after installation or when you want to renem your credentials)				     -
-                                                                                                            -
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
